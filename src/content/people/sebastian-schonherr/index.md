Sebastian Schönherr is a computational geneticist from Innsbruck (Austria) and co-leads a small research team which develops solutions for large-data genetics. He is also the co-creator of the Michigan Imputation Server, a large imputation web-service and developed several software packages for analyzing the mitochondrial DNA.

Sebastian will present a talk titled "Performing large and reproducible GWAS analysis on biobank-scale data".
